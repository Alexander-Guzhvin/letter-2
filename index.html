Global Head of Sales Ivan has been with Tools for Brokers since 2016, spending the last two years as the Head of Sales in the Cyprus office and helping expand TFB’s presence in multiple markets. With this new role, Ivan will focus on shaping and executing the company’s global sales strategy, working closely with the local teams and key clients worldwide.

With his recent promotion, Sergei will focus on strengthening the company’s presence in APAC and developing strong client and partner relationships.
Global Head of Partnerships Lola has been part of the TFB team since 2016 and has always shown a passion and talent for developing partner relationships and networking. In the new role as the Global Head of Partnerships, Lola will continue to nurture key partner relationships and build a strategy to strengthen TFB’s global network.
